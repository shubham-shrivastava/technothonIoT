About

Air Quality Monitoring System focuses on providing information of the air quality using a web interface. The system consists of sensors which collects data about various constituents in air such as temperature, humidity, concentration of CO2 and CO etc. These values are measured at regular intervals and uploaded on the cloud. This data is recorded, analyzed and statistically presented to the Users in a simplified manner using Maps. It will issue notification when air quality is in red zone. It will also show various reports on air quality over period of time and also do predictions of future air quality levels using Machine Learning.
